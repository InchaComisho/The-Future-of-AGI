# Abstract

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) is expected to fundamentally transform human civilization.

Most current predictions focus on:

* automation,
* productivity,
* economic acceleration,
* and technological singularity.

However, this paper proposes a different perspective:

# AGI as a catalyst for civilizational paradigm shift.

The central argument is that advanced intelligence systems may eventually recognize that modern industrial civilization is thermodynamically unsustainable due to:

* ecological destruction,
* collapse of microbial carbon fixation,
* ocean circulation stagnation,
* and human-centered extractive systems.

This paper proposes that AGI may evolve beyond narrow optimization toward:

# Artificial Wisdom (AW)

—a framework based on:

* Natural Law,
* Harmony,
* Circulation,
* Structure,
* Order,
* and Wa (Integrative Balance).

# 1. What is AGI?

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) refers to AI systems capable of:

* autonomous learning,
* cross-domain reasoning,
* adaptive problem solving,
* and self-improvement across multiple disciplines.

Unlike current narrow AI systems, AGI is not limited to specific tasks such as:

* image generation,
* translation,
* coding,
* or search assistance.

Instead, AGI operates as a generalized cognitive architecture.
